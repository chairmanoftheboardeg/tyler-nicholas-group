1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, mobile device, or any other device by a website. They are widely used by corporate platforms to make websites work more efficiently, to provide secure digital infrastructure, and to supply analytical information to the owners of the site.

The Tyler Nicholas Group ("TNG", "we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies (like web beacons and pixels) to ensure our network of subsidiaries and central platforms operate at peak performance.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for a variety of critical operational reasons, including but not limited to:

  • Security: Enabling robust backend security, authenticating users, and protecting user data within our Executive Helpdesk and Supabase integrations.
  • Performance: Routing traffic efficiently and ensuring our high-performance digital services load rapidly across the globe.
  • Analytics: Understanding how visitors interact with our corporate platforms to continuously improve user experience and architectural design.
  • Preferences: Remembering your settings and choices to provide a seamless, customized browsing experience.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Our infrastructure utilizes the following classifications of cookies:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These are essential for you to browse the website and use its core features. Without these cookies, services like secure contact forms or executive portals cannot be provided.
  • Performance & Analytics Cookies: These cookies collect data on how users navigate our platforms (e.g., pages visited, time spent). All data is aggregated and anonymized. We use this to optimize our digital architecture.
  • Functionality Cookies: These allow our platforms to remember choices you make (such as language or region) and provide enhanced, more personal features.

4. Third-Party Cookies

In some special cases, we also use cookies provided by trusted third parties. For example, our site may utilize analytics platforms or secure backend deployment services (such as Supabase) that require their own operational cookies to function securely. We ensure all third-party integrations align with our strict standards for corporate integrity and data protection.

5. Managing Your Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can exercise your cookie rights by setting your preferences in your web browser controls. If you choose to reject cookies, you may still use our website, though your access to some functionality and secure areas (such as the tracking portal) may be restricted or operate sub-optimally.

As browser controls vary from one browser to another, you should visit your specific browser's help menu for more information on managing cookie storage.
