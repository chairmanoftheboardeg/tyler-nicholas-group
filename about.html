Our Story

Tyler Nicholas Group began as a decision to treat online ventures with the same structure and attention as a traditional corporate group. Rather than operating separate projects in isolation, the vision was to create a unified digital holdings company with a clear name, identity, and long-term plan.

The early portfolio started with gaming, e-commerce, and foundation concepts. Today, TNG serves as the parent entity for current and future digital businesses that sit under the group umbrella.

From Ideas to a Group

What began as separate ideas became a structured portfolio. Each initiative is now designed to align with a wider group strategy, not just exist on its own.

Corporate Mindset

TNG is operated with a corporate mindset from day one—governance, naming, and clarity of purpose are part of the foundation, not an afterthought.

Purpose

The purpose of Tyler Nicholas Group is to give digital businesses a professional home: a place where ideas can be developed, governed, and grown over time under one parent company.

TNG exists to think long-term—beyond a single game, store, or project—by shaping a portfolio that can evolve as platforms and opportunities change.

Why We Exist

To create a disciplined, corporate-grade environment for building and operating online ventures that are intended to last.

How We Deliver

By combining creative digital ideas with clear roadmaps, shared standards, and a group-level strategy.

Working at Tyler Nicholas Group

As the portfolio grows, TNG aims to build a professional and respectful environment for collaborators, contractors, and future employees. The focus is on clarity, ownership, and growth.

Work at TNG is remote-friendly and built around digital operations: from Roblox development and e-commerce management to community building and foundation support.

Culture

A structured, serious culture where people are treated professionally and are able to connect their work to long-term group goals.

Future Opportunities

As ventures launch and scale, TNG expects to open roles across design, operations, digital marketing, and impact work tied to The Nicholas Foundation Seychelles.
