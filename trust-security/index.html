Trust & Security Center

Protecting our users, empowering our communities, and upholding the highest ethical standards across the global Tyler Nicholas Group ecosystem.

Ethics & Whistleblower Protection

TNG is committed to an open culture. Employees, volunteers, and users can safely report Code of Conduct violations, harassment, or financial impropriety directly to our executive board without fear of retaliation.

Community & Child Safety

Operating large-scale virtual simulations requires immense responsibility. Backed by Blackwood Digital Services, we enforce zero-tolerance policies for bullying, ensuring a safe digital environment for all ages.

Cybersecurity & Infrastructure

From the Falcon LTD digital storefront to BlueWave Digital's agency servers, we employ enterprise-grade security protocols to protect user data, financial transactions, and corporate intellectual property.

Vulnerability Disclosure

We welcome ethical hackers and developers to responsibly report security flaws or exploits within our web infrastructure or game servers before they can be utilized maliciously.
