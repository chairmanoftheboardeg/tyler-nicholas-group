Corporate Status

The Tyler Nicholas Group (TNG) is a privately held global holding company registered and headquartered in the Republic of Seychelles. TNG serves as the central administrative and governing body for a diversified portfolio of digital and on-site subsidiaries.

Each subsidiary within the Tyler Nicholas Group—including Falcon LTD, BlueWave Digital, Blackwood Digital Services, and our various virtual aviation entities—operates as a distinct business vertical under the overarching governance and strategic direction of the TNG Executive Board.
