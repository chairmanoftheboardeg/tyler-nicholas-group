Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026 • Tyler Nicholas Group LLC

The Tyler Nicholas Group ("TNG", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is fundamentally committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy informs you how we safeguard your information when you visit our corporate website, apply for a career, or interact with our diverse portfolio of global subsidiaries.

By using our services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. The Data We Collect

Personal data means any information about an individual from which that person can be identified. We may collect, use, store, and transfer different kinds of personal data, grouped as follows:

  • Identity Data: First name, last name, usernames, or similar identifiers utilized across our virtual platforms (e.g., Emirates Group Roblox).
  • Contact Data: Email addresses, phone numbers, and communication preferences provided via our Contact or Trust & Security portals.
  • Professional Data: Resumes, CVs, portfolio links, and employment history submitted through our Careers or Corporate Alumni portals.
  • Technical Data: Internet protocol (IP) address, browser type and version, time zone setting, and operating system used to access our platforms.
  • Transaction Data: Details about payments and digital assets purchased through our e-commerce subsidiaries (e.g., Falcon LTD). Note: Payment processing is handled by secure third-party gateways; TNG does not store raw credit card data.

2. How We Use Your Data

We will only use your personal data when international law allows us to. Most commonly, we will use your personal data in the following circumstances:

  • To process job applications, alumni registry requests, or corporate partnership inquiries.
  • To deliver digital products, services, and technical support requested through our subsidiaries.
  • To investigate confidential reports submitted to our Trust & Security Center.
  • To maintain the safety, security, and integrity of our virtual aviation communities and ecosystems.
  • To comply with a legal or regulatory obligation.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

As a holding company, data may be securely shared internally between the Tyler Nicholas Group Executive Board and the leadership teams of our specific subsidiaries (e.g., BlueWave Digital, FoodShare Seychelles) to facilitate operations, recruitment, or internal investigations.

We do not sell your personal data to external marketing agencies. We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers (such as hosting platforms, analytics providers, or payment gateways) who are bound by strict confidentiality and data protection agreements.

4. Data Security & Retention

We have implemented enterprise-grade security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, altered, disclosed, or accessed in an unauthorized way. Access to personal data is strictly limited to employees, agents, and contractors who have a critical business need to know.

We will only retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes we collected it for, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

5. Your Legal Rights

Depending on your geographical location (such as under the GDPR in Europe), you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction of any incomplete or inaccurate data we hold about you.
  • Request erasure of your personal data when there is no good reason for us continuing to process it.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where we are relying on consent to process your personal data.

Questions Regarding Our Privacy Practices?

If you wish to exercise your legal rights or have questions regarding how TNG handles your data, please contact our corporate legal team.
