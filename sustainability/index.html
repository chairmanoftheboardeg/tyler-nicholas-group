Sustainability & ESG

Driving growth with responsibility. Our commitment to environmental stewardship, social equity, and principled governance.

Our Impact Strategy

The Tyler Nicholas Group (TNG) recognizes that our long-term success is intrinsically linked to the health of the planet and the prosperity of the communities in which we operate. As a global holding company, we utilize our influence to ensure that every subsidiary adheres to rigorous ESG standards.

We don't just build for today; we govern for tomorrow.

TNG ESG Commitments

  • Zero-Tolerance for modern slavery and unethical labor.
  • Direct funding for Seychelles conservation initiatives.
  • Carbon-conscious digital infrastructure and cloud hosting.
  • Transparent executive reporting and third-party audits.

Environmental

Despite our digital focus, we prioritize low-impact infrastructure. We support the Tyler Nicholas Foundation in its mission to protect the unique biodiversity of the Seychelles.

Social

We believe in human potential. Through FoodShare Seychelles and our inclusive global hiring practices, we actively fight poverty and foster community growth across borders.

Governance

Our Governance Framework ensures absolute transparency, board-level accountability, and strict adherence to international legal standards.

The Tyler Nicholas Foundation

As the non-profit pillar of the TNG Group, the Foundation manages our philanthropic investments in environmental conservation and youth empowerment in the Seychelles.
