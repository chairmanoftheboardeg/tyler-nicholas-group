What is Tyler Nicholas Group?

Tyler Nicholas Group (TNG) brings multiple online businesses together under one professional parent company. Instead of treating each project as a one-off idea, TNG gives them structure, governance, and a clear future.

Our ambition is to grow into a portfolio holding dozens of digital businesses: virtual aviation experiences, curated e-commerce brands, and impact-focused foundations. Everything we build is designed to feel intentional, long-term, and part of something bigger.

TNG acts like the “corporate layer” above each business, setting standards for quality, conduct, and the way we show up online as a group.

Structured Like a Group

We think in terms of a group of companies, not just standalone projects. Each brand has its own name and audience, but sits within a single corporate family.

Built for Scale

From the start, TNG is designed to scale into a multi-business portfolio with consistent standards, shared services, and a clear identity.

Initial Portfolio Pillars

We are starting with three clear pillars that define the first phase of the group. Over time, each pillar will grow into multiple businesses and initiatives.

Gaming & Virtual Experiences

Emirates Group Roblox

A Roblox-based ecosystem inspired by aviation and travel. Players experience virtual airports, airline operations, and immersive roleplay, built with structure and quality in mind.

E-commerce

AlignX

A Shopify brand created as part of TNG’s commerce pillar. AlignX focuses on curated digital-era products and a clean customer journey, with room to expand into multiple product lines.

Foundations

Purpose-Led Work

The Nicholas Foundation Seychelles and a future Roblox venture capital-style foundation bring social impact, opportunity, and community support directly into the heart of the group’s strategy.
