Accessibility Statement

Last Updated

April 23, 2026

1. Our Commitment

The Tyler Nicholas Group ("TNG") is firmly committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone across our holding company platforms and subsidiary networks, and we actively apply the relevant accessibility standards to achieve this goal.

We believe that the internet should be available and accessible to anyone, and we are committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of circumstance and ability.

2. Conformance Status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) define requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. It defines three levels of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA.

The Tyler Nicholas Group corporate website is actively targeting partial conformance with WCAG 2.1 Level AA. We are currently auditing our backend UI frameworks, interactive data components, and executive portals to ensure they meet these rigorous standards.

3. Accessibility Measures

TNG takes the following measures to ensure the accessibility of our digital infrastructure:

  • Clear Navigation: Ensuring our digital architecture provides a consistent, predictable, and keyboard-navigable structure.
  • Visual Contrast: Utilizing high-contrast color palettes (such as TNG Navy and White) to improve readability for users with visual impairments.
  • Text Alternatives: Providing descriptive alt-text for corporate imagery and subsidiary portfolio logos.
  • Continuous Review: Integrating accessibility checks into our standard Web deployment protocols.

4. Known Limitations

Despite our best efforts to ensure accessibility of the Tyler Nicholas Group website, there may be some limitations. Below is a description of known limitations. Please contact us if you observe an issue not listed below.

  • Third-Party Integrations: Some of our subsidiary portals or external funding links may rely on third-party frameworks that we do not directly control. We cannot guarantee full WCAG compliance on external domains.
  • Archived Documents: Older PDF press releases or text documents may not yet be fully optimized for screen readers.

5. Feedback & Support

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of the Tyler Nicholas Group website. If you encounter accessibility barriers on our digital platforms, please let us know immediately so our technical team can address the issue.
