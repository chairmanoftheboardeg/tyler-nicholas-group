About Us

A registered LLC and dynamic holding company driving innovation across diverse global and local markets.

Who We Are

The Tyler Nicholas Group is a premier holding and parent company headquartered in the Seychelles, operating a vast portfolio of digital and on-site ventures worldwide. Founded by Tyler Nicholas, TNG was established to bring centralized, strategic oversight to a rapidly expanding network of independent businesses.

As a registered LLC tailored for the modern digital economy, we leverage our extensive corporate infrastructure to nurture growth, enforce high-level governance, and maximize operational synergy across all our subsidiaries.

Our Operational Reach

  • Global & Local: Operating worldwide while maintaining deep roots and vital on-site services within the Seychelles.
  • Registered Entity: A fully registered LLC compliant with international standards.
  • Strategic Synergy: Providing shared services like marketing, security, and infrastructure.

A Message From Our Founder

Our Purpose & Story

The Tyler Nicholas Group was born out of a necessity for structured growth. As individual projects began to scale rapidly, the need for a unified corporate umbrella became clear. Our purpose is to incubate, manage, and elevate ventures by providing them with the executive oversight and capital allocation they need to thrive.

Phase 1

The Genesis

The initial launch of our core standalone projects, establishing a strong digital footprint and active user bases in the virtual aviation and gaming sectors.

Phase 2

Formation of TNG

Tyler Nicholas Group is officially established as a registered LLC to act as the parent company, consolidating governance, security, and shared services.

Phase 3

Diversification

Expansion into e-commerce, digital tools via Falcon Ltd, and social impact initiatives in the Seychelles.
