Who We Are

The Tyler Nicholas Group is a premier holding and parent company headquartered in the Seychelles, operating a vast portfolio of digital and on-site ventures worldwide. Founded by Tyler Nicholas, TNG was established to bring centralized, strategic oversight to a rapidly expanding network of independent businesses.

As a registered LLC tailored for the modern digital economy, we leverage our extensive corporate infrastructure to nurture growth, enforce high-level governance, and maximize operational synergy across all our subsidiaries.

Our Operational Reach

  • Global & Local: Operating worldwide while maintaining deep roots and vital on-site services within the Seychelles.
  • Registered Entity: A fully registered LLC compliant with international standards.
  • Strategic Synergy: Providing shared services like marketing, security, and infrastructure.
